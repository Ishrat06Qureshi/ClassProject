FAQ

Is this program self-paced?

No. The React Nanodegree Program has a fixed start and end dates, and you must complete all assigned projects by the Term End Date. However, projects may be submitted at any time during a Term, and individual project deadlines are recommendations, not requirements. So within a Term, there is some opportunity to work at your own pace. But you should plan to follow our recommended timeline, as this will best enable you to keep pace with your peers and complete the program on time.

What are my options if I don’t complete the program on time?

To resume access to the course, you would need to re-enroll in a new term and pay the associated enrollment fees again. Your coursework progress and your project progress will be saved, so you can start exactly where you left off.
